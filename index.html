Driftwood Tavern

Tavern & inn

Tavern

At Driftwood Tavern, we extend a warm invitation for you to immerse yourself in a festive atmosphere. Indulge in our exquisite dishes, including vegan options to satisfy even the most discerning druids. You can also enjoy our refreshing beverages, with non-alcoholic alternatives (perfect for those looking to avoid constitution saves). While you share tales of your adventures, our comfortable tables and lively buzz create the perfect setting to recharge and toast to the victories of the day. Welcome to a feast of flavors and camaraderie!"

Inn

Is the party feeling exhausted after a day filled with quests? At Driftwood Tavern, we offer an inn area with private rooms and cozy beds, perfect for enjoying a long rest and replenishing those spent spell slots in total comfort.

Live Shows

In search of inspiration? Allow us to take you on a journey of wonders every night. On our vibrant stage, bardic performances unfold with musical magic and captivating tales. Immerse yourself in the harmony of melodies that enchant the soul and stories that awaken the imagination.

Stables

At Driftwood Tavern, we not only create a warm and festive atmosphere but also care for the needs of your loyal four-legged companions. Our stables are an oasis of safety and care for the intrepid adventurers who arrive with their faithful mounts. Whether it's a trusted horse, an exotic camel, a majestic elephant, or even the shape-shifting druid with unique tastes, our stables are designed to accommodate them all.

Potions Catalog

Facing the imposing threat of chromatic dragons and realizing you're out of resistance potions? Or perhaps the idea of gracefully navigating castle walls with ease using a flight potion seems more appealing? At Driftwood Tavern, we offer a diverse catalog of potions to replenish your inventory, preparing you for the challenges that await on the rugged Sword Coast. Whatever your need, we're here to ensure you're fully equipped and ready to face any adversity.
